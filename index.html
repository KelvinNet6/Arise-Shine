Your Journey

Discover the meaning of Olimi, our story of healing, coaching, and spirituality. Olimi represents a path of spiritual enlightenment and transformation. It is more than just a name; it embodies the essence of alignment, peace, and growth. Our journey began with a deep desire to help others discover their inner peace and heal from emotional and spiritual wounds.


Through years of coaching, self-discovery, and spiritual practices, we’ve created a space where individuals can come together, heal, grow, and find alignment in their lives. At Olimi, our core values guide everything we do: Peace, Alignment, Growth, Love, and Freedom. We are committed to continual personal growth, breaking free from limiting beliefs, and creating a life of abundance and joy. Join us on this transformative journey and empower yourself to live a life that’s aligned with your true purpose.

About Us

Olimi Arise and Shine empowers you with pathways to personal growth. Our vision is to see individuals and families restored, aligned, and walking in true freedom. Explore our programs to start your journey today.

Healing +

Deep inner healing and restoration. This pathway is designed to help you heal emotionally, spiritually, and mentally. Through therapeutic practices and self-reflection, you’ll learn how to release old wounds, negative patterns, and limiting beliefs that prevent you from living your fullest life.


We guide you through powerful healing techniques such as journaling, energy work, and mindfulness that allow you to reconnect with your authentic self. Healing is a journey of self-compassion and forgiveness, and we are here to support you every step of the way.


Our goal is to help you restore balance in your life, heal past trauma, and create a life filled with peace, love, and vitality. By embracing this pathway, you will discover the power of healing to transform your life and relationships, leading you toward true wholeness.

Peace +

Daily practices to find peace within. This pathway focuses on cultivating inner peace through mindfulness, meditation, and daily rituals that bring you into harmony with your inner self. Peace isn’t something that comes and goes; it's something you can create and maintain every day.


Through guided practices and techniques, you will learn how to calm your mind, reduce stress, and embrace tranquility, no matter what life throws your way. It’s about finding peace in the present moment and letting go of the need to control everything.


Our mission is to help you build a peaceful foundation that allows you to navigate life’s challenges with a calm and centered mind. The more you practice, the more peace you will experience in every aspect of your life, from relationships to career to personal growth.

Freedom +

Break free from limitations and live freely. This pathway is about identifying and overcoming the barriers that hold you back from living a life of abundance and fulfillment. We guide you in shedding limiting beliefs, fear, and negative thought patterns that restrict your growth.


Through mindfulness, self-reflection, and actionable steps, you'll discover how to embrace true freedom in all areas of your life. Our goal is to help you create space for personal growth, letting go of the past, and stepping into a future filled with endless possibilities.


Freedom isn’t just about breaking physical chains; it’s about freeing your mind and spirit to fully live the life you’ve always dreamed of. Let us show you how to overcome internal and external obstacles to living freely and joyfully.

Finance +

Empowering your financial journey and mindset. In this pathway, we focus on overcoming financial challenges, shifting limiting beliefs around money, and building a strong foundation for financial success.



Through practical tools and strategies, you'll gain clarity on budgeting, saving, and investing, as well as a deeper understanding of how to create lasting wealth. Our goal is to help you transform your relationship with money and move toward a life of financial freedom.


Whether you're just starting out or looking to optimize your financial future, this program will help you set clear goals and take actionable steps to achieve them. Let us guide you in creating a sustainable and abundant financial future.

Pathways to Shine

Explore our transformational pathways: Self-Healing, Spiritual Alignment, Financial Freedom, Marriage & Relationships, and Personal Peace. Each path is designed to empower you to take charge of your life and grow in various aspects, from emotional healing to financial clarity.


Self-Healing focuses on inner restoration through practices like mindfulness and self-love, while Spiritual Alignment helps you find balance and connection with your higher self. We believe that a clear spiritual path can lead to greater peace and purpose in your life.


Financial Freedom is about breaking the chains of financial stress, building wealth, and adopting a healthy mindset for abundance. In Marriage & Relationships, we offer tools for couples to grow together in love, forgiveness, and trust. Finally, Personal Peace provides rituals and practices that help you maintain peace in everyday life, no matter the challenges you face.
